Spaces, Functions and Numbers Seminar

Welcome to Spaces, Functions and Numbers Seminar! This is a general-audience colloquium series for all members of the Sydney math community, including undergraduates at any level.

When: Tuesday, 4:30 - 6:00 pm AEDT for in-person talks; 10:00 - 11:30 am AEST for Zoom talks.

Where: In-person at SMRI Seminar Room at Macleay Building A12 Room 301 or Zoom Meeting.

Please ensure you are logged in with your University Gmail account before joining the Zoom link.

To subscribe to our mailing list email the organizer: june.park [at] sydney.edu.au


2025 Semester 1 Schedule:

  • Tuesday, 25th February (In-person): June Park (The University of Sydney)

    Analogy between polynomials and integers as global sections

  • Tuesday, 4th March (In-person): June Park (The University of Sydney)

    Inner arithmetic of rational points on moduli stacks

  • Tuesday, 11th March (Zoom): Tristan Phillips (Dartmouth College)

    Equidistribution of rational points on weighted projective stacks

  • Tuesday, 18th & 25th March: Break due to MATRIX workshop - Talk slides.

  • Tuesday, 1st April (Zoom): Dori Bejleri (University of Maryland, College Park)

    Compact moduli and degenerations

  • Tuesday, 8th April (Zoom): Monica Kang (University of Pennsylvania)

    Finding isomorphic quantum field theories

  • Tuesday, 15th April (Zoom): Jungin Lee (Ajou University)

    Distribution of the cokernels of random p-adic matrices

  • Tuesday, 22nd April: Mid–semester University Break.

  • Tuesday, 6th May (In-person): Scott Mullane (The University of Melbourne)

    Teichmüller dynamics and the moduli space of curves

    Special time and place : 10-11:30 am at SMRI Seminar Room.

  • Tuesday, 5th May to 13th June: SMRI Special Semester Modern perspectives in representation theory.
