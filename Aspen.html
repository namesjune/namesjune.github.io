Spaces, Functions and Numbers Seminar

Welcome to Spaces, Functions and Numbers Seminar! This is a general-audience colloquium series for all members of the Sydney math community, including undergraduates at any level.

When: Tuesday, 4:30 - 6:00 pm AEDT for in-person talks; 10:00 - 11:30 am AEDT for Zoom talks.

Where: In-person at SMRI Seminar Room at Macleay Building A12 Room 301 or Zoom Meeting.

Please ensure you are logged in with your University Gmail account before joining the Zoom link.

To subscribe to our mailing list email the organizer: june.park [at] sydney.edu.au
