Title: Unity of polynomial and integer algebra
 
 
Abstract: In this talk, I will explain the basic analogy between polynomial functions and integer numbers through the idea of "global sections over a global field".
 
I will start from the simple example of x^2 + y^2 = 1 over Q (Pythagorean triple numbers) and look at the analogous problem of Hom_n(P^1,P^1) (Space of rational functions).
